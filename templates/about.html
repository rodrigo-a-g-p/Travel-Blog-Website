Hey there! My name is Rodrigo and I wrote this little blog as a proposed project from a programming course I took. I wrote about these three countries because, in my opinion, these are the most culturally rich countries in the world. Personally, I have visited both Egypt and Mexico, but never visited India.

In fact, I am planning on doing so this year (2023) as well as to visit Pakistan. My plan is to visit Delhi, Agra, Jaipur (in the tourist lexicon, these three cities make up the famous "Golden Triangle"), Amritsar, then I want to attend the India/Pakistan border-ceremony (on the Indian side), cross the border into Pakistan, visit Lahore, go back to the border to attend the ceremony on the Pakistani side, and then visit Mohenjo-Daro (probably the oldest city ever), and finally, visit Karachi. Let's see how it goes!

I was planning on adding China to the blog, but I feel like China is extremely overwhelming, and I would not know how to introduce it properly. Maybe when I am better versed in the history and culture of China, I'll add it to the blog.

UPDATE May 1st, 2024:

It has been over a year since I first pushed this project into a repo in late january 2023. Since then, from late march to late april 2023, I travelled to India, and Pakistan (and also Istanbul, Turkey), and it was an amazing trip. I did everything I wrote above, except for re-watching the border ceremony, this time on the Pakistani side, as I thought it would be the same thing again (after having watched from the Indian side). Next time, I'll attend the ceremony from the Pakistani side. I have not travelled in over a year because I am doing an orthodontic treatment, which started in late August 2023. Meanwhile, the next trips I have planned are the following: China, Iran, Southeast Asia (Vietnam, a bit of Laos, Cambodia, and a stop in Mumbai), Latin America (Colombia, Peru, and Guatemala), and Pakistan-Afghanistan. I'll keep you updated.

Cheers!
